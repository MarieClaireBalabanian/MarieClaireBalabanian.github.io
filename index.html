Sound*Swap

Concept/Design/Coding (2017)

Being a touring musician can get complicated. Amps break in the middle of Who-Knows-Where, Oklahoma. And can your van really fit that big old Rhodes piano? Sound Swap is where local musicians help touring musicians. Need some gear? Locate available musical equipment in any US city your tour takes you through. Borrow from local musicians. Pay it forward and share with touring musicians.

Built with: HTML, CSS, MaterializeCSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Node.js, Express.js, Handlebars.js, Bookshelf.js, Knex, SQLite3.
