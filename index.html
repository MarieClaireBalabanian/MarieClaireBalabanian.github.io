About Me

I'm a Chicago-based Junior Web Developer with a passion for creating and connecting. A graduate of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program, I have a deep interest in both Front End and Back End Development. My ultimate goal is to work with other creative and technical minds to build beautiful things that enrich the lives of users. My skills include HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, Node.js, Express, Ruby, Sinatra, Mongodb and Sqlite3. When I'm not coding, I'm creating blissed out soundscapes with my band, volunteering at my favorite local animal shelter or getting lost in nature.
